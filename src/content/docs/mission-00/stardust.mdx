**Stardust** is the Academy's primary reward currency. It is awarded to pilots who demonstrate consistency and technical proficiency.

You earn Stardust by *doing the work*:

-   **Simulations:** Successfully running Holodeck queries.
-   **Mission Completion:** Finalizing training objectives.
-   **Daily Logins:** Maintaining your streak to earn multiplier bonuses.

## Feedback Loop

Stardust provides a critical feedback loop for your training:

-   **Validation:** Learn a concept, execute a query and receive immediate confirmation of success.
-   **Momentum:** Building a daily streak amplifies your rewards, encouraging consistent flight time.
