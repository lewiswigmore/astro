## Target Coordinates

You will frequently intercept signals from these primary tables:

-   `AuthenticationEvents`: Records of personnel login attempts.
-   `Email`: Communication logs across the network.
-   `Employees`: The central crew manifest.
-   `FileCreationEvents`: Records of new files generated on systems.
-   `OutboundBrowsing`: Logs of external web traffic.
-   `PassiveDns`: DNS resolution history.
-   `ProcessEvents`: Execution logs of system processes.
-   `InboundBrowsing`: Logs of incoming web traffic.

## Flight Rules

-   **Public Frequency:** Treat the `help` cluster as a public space. Never transmit classified data or secrets.
-   **Efficiency:** Prefer using **time filters** and the `take` operator when exploring new sectors to avoid overloading your sensors.

## Fuel Management

Executing queries in the **Holodeck** or via the **Uplink** consumes your vessel's fuel.

-   **Passive Regeneration:** Your fuel cells automatically recharge at a rate of **1% per minute**.
-   **Fuel Breaks:** If you run low, you can initiate a **Fuel Break** (available every 30 minutes) to instantly restore a significant portion of your reserves.
