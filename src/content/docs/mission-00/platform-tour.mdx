The Academy is divided into two primary operational stations.

## Command Center

The **Cockpit** is your central launchpad and command center:

-   **Pilot Card:** Monitor your level, rank and total Stardust.
-   **Status Indicators:** Track your **Daily Streak** and **Activity** grid to maintain momentum.
-   **Mission Control:** Select your next training objective.

## Research Deck

The **Observatory** is your dedicated study bay and research deck:

-   **Mission Logs:** Access detailed lessons and documentation.
-   **Holodeck Simulations:** Preview KQL within the logs to prepare your **Uplink** (no live execution).
-   **Skill Constellations:** Visualize your progression through the KQL mastery tree.
