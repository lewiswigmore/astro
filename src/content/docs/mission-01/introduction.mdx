Welcome to the cockpit, Cadet. You are about to embark on a journey through the **Kusto Nebula**. Your vessel is powered by **KQL (Kusto Query Language)**, the most efficient propulsion system for navigating massive streams of telemetry data.

In this first session, our objective is to get you flight-ready. By the end of this mission, you will be able to:

*   **Navigate** the data galaxy (Tables & Columns)
*   **Filter** out cosmic noise (Where & Project)
*   **Analyze** signal patterns (Summarize & Render)

## Target Coordinates

We will be conducting our maneuvers in the **SecurityLogs** sector, focusing on primary data streams:

*   `AuthenticationEvents`: Records of personnel accessing the ship's systems.
*   `FileCreationEvents`: Intercepting signals of new files generated across the network.
*   `Employees`: The central crew manifest.

We may also intercept signals from `InboundBrowsing` and `OutboundBrowsing` for advanced tactical training.
